Key Insights

Personal Dominates

26% of titles use personal framing ("My Life," "My Story"), showing politicians prefer intimate, relatable narratives over institutional accounts.

Hope vs. Struggle

Optimistic themes (19%) slightly outweigh struggle narratives (7%), suggesting politicians prefer to frame their stories positively.

Universal Patterns

Similar themes appear across different countries and eras, indicating universal approaches to political storytelling.
